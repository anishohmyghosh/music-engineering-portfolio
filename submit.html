Submit Your Project

This page walks you through the entire submission process: prepare your project details, generate your markdown, upload your images, create your project file, and open your pull request.

Step 1: Before You Start

  • Pick one project type: Research, Recording, Project, Capstone, or Demo.
  • Add your course if your project belongs to one, for example `MUE410`.
  • Prepare all project imagery, including your thumbnail, screenshots, diagrams, and any images you want to show inside the full description.
  • Recommended image size: JPG or PNG, about 1600 x 900 px for thumbnails, under 2 MB when possible.
  • Have your repository link ready, plus an optional demo link.

Step 2: Fill Out Your Project Details

  1. Fork the repository: github.com/MUEatTheU/music-engineering-portfolio

Fill out the form below and click Generate Markdown. The generated output is what you will paste into your new file on GitHub.

Project Details Form

To embed an image in your description, use Markdown like ![Plugin UI](/assets/images/projects/my-plugin-ui.png).

Upload every image you reference to assets/images/projects/ first, then use that exact path in your description.
